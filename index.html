Score Keeper Help

1. Select an avatar and enter your name in the "Enter Player Name" field.

2. Do the same for your opponent.

3. Choose "Total Rounds" from the drop-down menu.

4. Enter your scores in the "Woman Avatar Score" and "Man Avatar Score" fields.

5. Both fields must be filled in order to submit your scores with either "Submit Button" or hitting the "Enter" key.

6. When total rounds are completed the score will be automatically calculated and displayed.

7. If no "Total Rounds" are selected you can use "Current Total" button to display the total score.

8. Check highest and lowest scores from all completed sessions with "All Time Stats" button.

9. Click on "Reset Game" button to start a new game session.
